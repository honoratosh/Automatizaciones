# My Consulting Practice at i2c

I am a process and software consultant working with clients who need to organize their processes, data, systems and people roles. As part of the automation of their business, I can apply AI in some specific parts of their processes. I work with a team at the company i2c (information, intelligence and knowledge). The company's website is at i2c.mx.
My role is to work as a solutions architect and resolve technical problems.
Mainly, the job consists in the revision of the processes, documentation and implementation of ERPs like Odoo (odoo.com).

i2c is a firm specialized in the implementation of orchestrated Artificial Intelligence for organizations, focused on integrating AI capabilities within the real processes of the business and not as isolated tools.

Our team combines experience in software development, technological architecture and data analysis, with training in engineering, administration and data science. This technical base is complemented by practical experience in organizational process improvements, IT governance, and implementation of technological solutions.

Throughout our careers we have participated in process improvement initiatives in the software industry, working under quality models such as CMMI, where we have directed improvement plans that allowed organizations to reach maturity levels 2, 3, and 5.
